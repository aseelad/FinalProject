U.S.-led airstrikes target Islamic State leaders in Iraq: report | ReutersBAGHDAD (Reuters) - U.S.-led airstrikes targeted a gathering of Islamic State leaders in Iraq, possibly including the group's top man, Abu Bakr al-Baghdadi, Al-Hadath television station reported on Saturday.

Iraqi security officials were not immediately available for comment on the report from the station, part of Saudi-owned al-Arabiya television.
