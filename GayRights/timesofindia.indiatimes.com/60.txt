Megastar Amitabh Bachchan is left wondering about the consequences of the recent Supreme Court ruling that consensual sex between adults of the same gender is an offence.The 71-year-old took to his blog Wednesday night to share his thoughts on the ruling."The Supreme Court has passed judgment that homosexuality is a crime...and that has triggered a massive reaction among those that have till now had the preference of choice, as per human rights, to lead their life the way they wish to...I wonder where this shall lead to," Big B posted on blog.Several big names from the film industry like Aamir Khan Anupam Kher , Kalki Koechlin, Riteish Deshmukh, Neha Dhupia and Rahul Bose had also posted their disappointment over the ruling on micro-blogging site Twitter Wednesday.
