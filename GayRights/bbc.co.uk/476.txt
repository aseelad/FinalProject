Nicky Campbell asks- Are you a bigot if you oppose gay marriage? 

Nick Clegg insists he'd never describe opponents of gay marriage as "bigots" - after a draft of a speech he was due to give was leaked - which had him doing exactly that. A new version has now been released, replacing the word "bigot" with the less controversial phrase "some people".

Listen out for guests including gay rights campaigner Peter Tatchell and Peter Bone the Conservative MP.
